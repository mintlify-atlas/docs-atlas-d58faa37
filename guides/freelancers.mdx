# Freelancer Guide

Welcome to Khedma Market! This guide will help you establish your freelance business, create compelling service offerings, and successfully manage client relationships.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for a Khedma Market account and select "Freelancer" as your role during onboarding. You'll be asked to provide basic information about yourself and your services.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    A complete profile builds trust with potential clients. Navigate to your profile settings to add:
    - Professional profile picture
    - Bio and description of your expertise
    - Contact information (phone, website)
    - Languages you speak
    - Location details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build Your Professional Profile">
    Add credentials that showcase your expertise:
    - **Education**: University degrees and academic achievements
    - **Certifications**: Professional certificates and training
    - **Skills**: Technical and soft skills with proficiency levels
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Clients are more likely to hire freelancers with complete profiles. Aim for 100% profile completion before creating your first gig.
</Tip>

## Setting Up Your Freelancer Profile

### Profile Description

Your profile description is your elevator pitch. It should clearly communicate:
- What services you offer
- Your areas of expertise
- Your experience level
- What makes you unique

Navigate to your username profile page to edit your description directly.

### Languages

Add all languages you can work in, along with your proficiency level:
- Native
- Fluent
- Conversational
- Basic

### Skills

Add relevant skills with their proficiency levels. Skills help clients find you when searching for specific expertise.

## Building Your Portfolio

Your portfolio showcases your best work to potential clients. Access it from **Dashboard > Portfolio**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Project">
    Click "Add New" and provide:
    - **Title**: A clear, descriptive name for the project
    - **Description**: Detailed information about the project, your role, and outcomes
    - **Gallery**: Upload images showcasing your work
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save as Draft">
    Save projects as drafts to work on them before publishing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish Your Project">
    Once ready, change the status to "Published" to make it visible on your public profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Include 3-5 portfolio projects that represent your best work and the services you want to be hired for.
</Tip>

## Creating and Managing Gigs

Gigs are the services you offer to clients. Each gig should focus on a specific service or deliverable.

### Creating Your First Gig

Navigate to **Dashboard > Gigs** and click "Add New".

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Information">
    - **Title**: Clear, keyword-rich title (e.g., "I will design a professional logo for your business")
    - **Category**: Select the most relevant category
    - **Description**: Detailed explanation using the rich text editor
    - **Tags**: Add relevant keywords to help clients find your gig
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Media">
    Add high-quality images or videos that showcase:
    - Examples of your work
    - What the client will receive
    - Your process or workflow
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Packages">
    Offer up to three pricing tiers:
    
    **Basic Package**
    - Entry-level offering
    - Lower price point
    - Essential deliverables
    
    **Standard Package**
    - Most popular option
    - Balanced price and value
    - Additional features
    
    **Premium Package**
    - Comprehensive solution
    - Highest value
    - All features included
    
    For each package, specify:
    - Price
    - Delivery time (in days)
    - Number of revisions
    - Description of what's included
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add FAQs (Optional)">
    Answer common questions clients might have about your service.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish Your Gig">
    Save as draft to review, or publish immediately to make it available to clients.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Orders

When a client purchases your gig, an order is created. Manage all orders from **Dashboard > Orders**.

### Order Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Order Notification">
    You'll receive a notification when a client places an order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Order Details">
    Check the package purchased, delivery deadline, and any special requirements from the client.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Processing">
    Mark the order as "Processing" and begin work. Communicate with the client if you need clarification.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Deliver Work">
    Upload deliverables and mark the order as complete before the deadline.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Client Approval">
    The client reviews your work and either approves it or requests revisions (if included in the package).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Order Complete">
    Once approved, the order is marked as completed and payment is released.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Order Statuses

- **Pending**: Order placed, awaiting your action
- **Processing**: You're actively working on the order
- **Completed**: Work delivered and approved by client
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled (rare)

<Warning>
  Always deliver work before the deadline. Late deliveries can result in negative reviews and impact your seller rating.
</Warning>

## Getting Paid

Khedma Market handles all payments securely. Here's how the payment process works:

### Payment Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Client Places Order">
    When a client purchases your gig, payment is held securely by Khedma Market.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the Work">
    Deliver your work according to the order requirements and deadline.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Client Approval">
    The client reviews and approves your work.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Payment Released">
    Once approved, payment is released to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tracking Earnings

View your earnings in **Dashboard > Earnings**:
- Total earnings
- Pending payments (work in progress)
- Available balance
- Payment history

### Withdrawal Methods

Set up your preferred payment method to withdraw earnings:
- Bank transfer
- PayPal
- Other payment methods (varies by region)

<Note>
  Payment processing times vary by method. Bank transfers typically take 3-5 business days.
</Note>

## Tips for Growing Your Business

1. **Start with competitive pricing** - Build your reputation before raising prices
2. **Specialize in a niche** - Become known for specific services
3. **Update regularly** - Keep your gigs and portfolio fresh
4. **Respond quickly** - Fast response times attract more clients
5. **Deliver extras** - Small bonuses create delighted customers
6. **Request feedback** - Use reviews to improve your services

<Tip>
  Your first 10 orders are crucial. Focus on delivering exceptional work to build a foundation of positive reviews.
</Tip>
