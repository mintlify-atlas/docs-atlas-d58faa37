# Client Guide

Welcome to Khedma Market! This guide will help you find talented freelancers, purchase services, and successfully manage your projects from start to finish.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for a Khedma Market account and select "Client" as your role during onboarding.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Add your basic information:
    - Name and contact details
    - Profile picture (optional but recommended)
    - Location
    - Brief description of your business or needs
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Browsing Services">
    Explore the service catalog to find freelancers who can help with your projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Understanding Gig Listings

Each service (called a "gig") shows:
- **Title**: Clear description of the service offered
- **Seller Information**: Freelancer's name, profile picture, and rating
- **Price**: Starting price for the basic package
- **Images**: Portfolio samples and service previews
- **Reviews**: Ratings and feedback from previous clients

<Tip>
  Look for freelancers with:
  - High ratings (4+ stars)
  - Multiple positive reviews
  - Recent activity
  - Complete profiles with portfolios
</Tip>

## Evaluating Freelancers

Before placing an order, thoroughly review the freelancer's profile and offerings.

### Check the Gig Details

Click on any gig to see:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Service Description">

    - What's included in the service
    - The freelancer's process
    - Any requirements from you
    - What you'll receive
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Package Options">
    Most gigs offer three tiers:
    
    **Basic** - Essential features at the lowest price
    
    **Standard** - Most popular, balanced option
    
    **Premium** - Comprehensive package with all features
    
    Compare what's included in each package:
    - Price
    - Delivery time

  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Gallery & Portfolio">
    Review images and samples to verify quality and style match your needs.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="FAQs">
    Check the FAQ section for answers to common questions about the service.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Pay attention to:

## Placing an Order

Once you've found the right service, placing an order is straightforward.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Package">
    Choose the package that best fits your needs (Basic, Standard, or Premium).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Order Details">
    Confirm:
    - Package selected
    - Total price
    - Delivery timeframe
    - What's included
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Requirements">
    If needed, provide:
    - Project details
    - Specific requirements
    - Reference materials
    - Any special instructions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Choose your payment method:
    - Credit/debit card
    - PayPal
    - Other available methods
    
    Your payment is held securely until you approve the completed work.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Order Confirmation">
    You'll receive confirmation and can track your order from your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your payment is protected. Funds are only released to the freelancer after you approve the completed work.
</Note>

### Working with Deliveries

When a freelancer delivers your order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the Work">
    Carefully review all deliverables:
    - Check against your requirements
    - Test functionality (if applicable)
    - Review quality and completeness
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request Revisions (If Needed)">
    If the work needs adjustments:
    - Be specific about what needs to change
    - Reference your original requirements
    - Be respectful and constructive
    - Check your package for number of included revisions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Approve the Work">
    If you're satisfied:
    - Click "Approve" or "Accept Delivery"
    - Payment is released to the freelancer
    - Order is marked as complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Leave a Review">
    Share your experience:
    - Rate the service (1-5 stars)
    - Write a detailed review
    - Mention specific positives
    - Help other clients make informed decisions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Review deliveries promptly. Freelancers appreciate quick feedback and it helps maintain project momentum.
</Tip>

## Managing Projects

### Working with Multiple Freelancers

For larger projects, you might hire multiple freelancers:

1. **Break down the project** into separate deliverables
2. **Order appropriate services** for each component
3. **Coordinate timelines** to ensure components align
4. **Manage dependencies** between different orders
5. **Consolidate deliverables** when everything is complete

## Common Questions

### What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

1. Request revisions if included in your package
2. Communicate specific concerns to the freelancer
3. If issues persist, contact Khedma Market support
4. Review the refund policy in case of non-delivery

### Can I cancel an order?

Cancellation policies vary:
- Before work begins: Usually possible with full refund
- Work in progress: Discuss with the freelancer
- Work delivered: Review and request revisions instead

### How do I get a refund?

Contact support if:
- Work was not delivered
- Deliverables don't match the description
- Quality issues cannot be resolved through revisions
