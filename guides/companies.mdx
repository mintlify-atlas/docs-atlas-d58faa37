# Company Guide

Welcome to Khedma Market! This guide will help you establish your company presence, post job openings, find talented freelancers, and build your team.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for a Khedma Market account and select "Company" as your role during onboarding.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Your Company Profile">
    You'll be directed to the company setup page where you'll provide essential information about your organization.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Posting Jobs">
    Once your company profile is complete, you can begin posting job openings and hiring freelancers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Complete your company profile before posting jobs. A professional company profile attracts higher-quality candidates.
</Tip>

## Creating Your Company Profile

Navigate to **Onboarding > Company Setup** to create your company profile.

    - Company culture highlights

### Company Description Best Practices

Your company description should include:

1. **What you do** - Core business and industry
2. **Company mission** - Purpose and values
3. **Team culture** - Work environment and culture
4. **Why join** - Benefits of working with your company
5. **Notable achievements** - Awards, recognitions, or milestones

<Note>
  Your company description appears on all your job postings. Make it compelling to attract top talent.
</Note>

## Posting Job Openings

Once your company profile is set up, you can post job opportunities for freelancers.

### Creating a Job Posting

Go to **Dashboard > Jobs** and click "Create a new job".

<Steps>
  <Step title="Job Title">
    Create a clear, specific job title that accurately describes the role:
    - Good: "Senior React Developer for E-commerce Platform"
    - Avoid: "Developer Needed" or "Urgent Hire"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Job Description">
    Use the rich text editor to create a comprehensive job description:
    
    **Include:**
    - Project or role overview
    - Key responsibilities
    - Required skills and qualifications
    - Preferred experience level
    - Project timeline or duration
    - Deliverables expected
    
    **Structure:**
    - Use headings and bullet points
    - Be specific about requirements
    - Highlight unique aspects of the project
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Job Details">
    Specify important details:
    
    **Job Type:**
    - Full-time
    - Part-time
    - Contract
    - Intern
    
    **Location:**
    - Specify city/country
    - Indicate if role can be remote
    
    **Salary/Budget:**
    - Provide salary range or project budget
    - Be transparent about compensation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Publish">
    Review all details carefully, then publish your job posting to make it visible to freelancers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Job Type Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Full-time" icon="briefcase">
    Permanent positions with regular hours and benefits. Ideal for core team members.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Part-time" icon="clock">
    Flexible positions with reduced hours. Great for specialized skills needed on an ongoing basis.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Contract" icon="file-contract">
    Project-based work with defined duration. Perfect for specific deliverables or temporary needs.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Intern" icon="graduation-cap">
    Entry-level positions for students or recent graduates. Build your talent pipeline.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Remote Work Options

Indicate if the position can be remote:
- **On-site only** - Candidate must work from your office
- **Remote possible** - Can work remotely full-time or hybrid
- **Remote-first** - Primarily remote with occasional on-site

<Tip>
  Jobs marked as remote-friendly receive significantly more applications. Consider remote options to access a wider talent pool.
</Tip>

## Hiring Process

Follow these steps to successfully hire freelancers through Khedma Market.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Shortlist Candidates">
    Review all applications and identify the most promising candidates based on:
    - Skills and experience
    - Portfolio quality
    - Cover letter fit
    - Availability and rates
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initial Contact">
    Reach out to shortlisted candidates:
    - Acknowledge their application
    - Express interest in their profile
    - Request additional information if needed
    - Schedule an interview or call
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Interview Process">
    Conduct interviews to assess:
    - Technical competency
    - Communication skills
    - Cultural fit
    - Project understanding
    - Availability and timeline
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check References">
    - Review ratings from previous clients
    - Read detailed reviews
    - Ask for references if needed
    - Verify claimed experience
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make an Offer">
    Once you've selected a candidate:
    - Clearly communicate the offer
    - Specify compensation and terms
    - Outline project timeline
    - Set clear expectations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Formalize Agreement">
    - Put terms in writing
    - Use Khedma Market's contract templates if available
    - Define deliverables and milestones
    - Establish communication cadence
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Onboard Successfully">
    - Provide necessary access and resources
    - Introduce to team members
    - Share relevant documentation
    - Set up first check-in meeting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Challenges and Solutions

### Finding Quality Candidates

**Challenge**: Too many unqualified applications

**Solutions**:
- Write more specific job descriptions
- List required skills clearly
- Include experience level requirements
- Mention must-have qualifications

### Managing Remote Teams

**Challenge**: Coordinating distributed freelancers

**Solutions**:
- Use project management tools
- Establish regular check-in schedules
- Set clear communication protocols
- Define working hours overlap

### Ensuring Quality

**Challenge**: Maintaining consistent quality

**Solutions**:
- Provide detailed briefs
- Share brand guidelines
- Review work at milestones
- Give constructive feedback
- Build trusted freelancer network

### Budget Management

**Challenge**: Controlling project costs

**Solutions**:
- Set clear budgets upfront
- Use fixed-price contracts when possible
- Define scope precisely
- Manage scope creep
- Track time and expenses

<Tip>
  Start with smaller projects to test freelancers before committing to larger, longer-term engagements.
</Tip>
